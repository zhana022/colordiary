Oct 4th

        The paper used, the font choice...
        the book's conception and layout also tell a story.

        Trees whisper within the book.
        Dust also holds weight within the book.


Oct 5th

        I'm going crazy if this keeps going...
        I used to be gentle and kind
        but because of you... 
        I've changed into a cold, cruel me...
        I've cast another spell on a doll that looks like you, 
        asking her to leave you... 
        -ABRACADABRA


Oct 6th

        Standing by the river, 
        quietly listening to the sound 
        of the flowing water and the wind.
    
        I gaze at the full moon in the sky.

        Dear family, halfway across the world, 
        we're looking at the same moon, 
        aren't we?
